Company Policy: Vacation Days
Employees are entitled to 15 vacation days per year.
Vacation requests must be submitted 2 weeks in advance.
Unused vacation days cannot be carried over to the next year.

Technical Support Information:
To reset the device, follow these steps:
1. Hold the power button for 10 seconds
2. Wait for the device to completely shut down
3. Press the power button once to restart
4. The device will perform a factory reset automatically
